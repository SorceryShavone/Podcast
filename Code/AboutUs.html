Shavone T.

A proud West-Indian descent, Shavone has always had a equity for women mindset. Her passion for women's health specifically began after witnessing the side effects of birth control can have on women. She currently wants to take her passion and seek for knowledge to help educate other women on general women's health, but also alternatives to the traditional approach.
